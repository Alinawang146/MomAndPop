About Us

Your local marketplace goes virtual with Mom & Pop! With user-friendly features and guides, small business owners are able to unlock a new threshhold of neighborhood regulars all at the click of a button.

Sustainability Pledge

Supporting local businesses translates into local purchases​, thus requiring less transportation. More broadly, shopping at Mom & Pop means less congestion, pollution, and waste.

Our COVID-19 Response

As the peak of the COVID-19 pandemic wanes, the E-Commerce industry continues to see a surge in activity. With Mom & Pop's universal platform, small businesses take advantage of this pivot, while still maintaining relevance within their local community.
